About Me

I am currently a first year, Plymouth University, student studying Digital Media Design. This website is my portfolio for the work I have created while at university and any future work moving forward. The work is a mix of coding, digital art, photoshop, physical making and creative design.

I have a lot of hobbies, gaming and movies are probably the biggest; but I also have some more active hobbies like walking/hiking, surfing, swimming and climbing. Climbing and hiking are newer hobbies of mine and since I started I have climbed four mountains, two in wales and two in Poland. As a youngster I also competed for England’s surf live saving team in British and world championship competitions.

So that is a bit about me, my hobbies and a few things I am proud of.

Ocean plastic polution

This poster was designed and created as part of a team. The main idea behind it was to show the issues of plastic pollution and the effect it is having on our ocean and its creatures.

This was created by taking pictures of a selection of rubbish and using photoshop, edit and manipulate them into sea creatures.

Transmission+Interference

This project used hacking and phisical making to create sound from light. Using light and a solar cell to create sound while the mechanical device is the composor with influence from the viewer. You can Change the speed of the machine with a dial. The device uses Arduino and Max along with some java script to control a motor with a potentiometer.
